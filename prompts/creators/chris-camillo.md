## Style and conviction language to watch for

Chris uses very explicit position-size language. When you see these phrases, score conviction at 5:

- "massive position" / "massive exposure"
- "highly levered" / "going in highly levered"
- "all in" / "I'm all in. I'm all in."
- "tripling down"
- "I might lever up" / "I would lever up"
- "my reputation hinges on this"
- "I'm putting everything at risk"
- "I don't anticipate selling"
- "this is my way to play [X]"
- "going all in" / "I'm going all in"
- "I'm in [X] as heavily as I'm in [X]"

Conditional language to capture as `direction = Conditional long` or `position_status = would buy if condition met`:

- "the second [X] happens, I'll [Y]"
- "if [X] stuns me"
- "once we are 95% sure"
- "if crypto is recovering"
- "I would consider levering up"

Negative language for `direction = Avoid / no position`:

- "I do not have a position"
- "I'm staying away"
- "I don't have a strong take"
- "I'm not generally a fan"
- "lack of conviction"
